Ingredients

  • 4 slices bread (sourdough, whole wheat, or your choice)
  • 4-6 ounces sliced deli meat (turkey, ham, roast beef, or chicken)
  • 2 slices cheese (Swiss, cheddar, provolone, or your choice)
  • 2 lettuce leaves (romaine, butter lettuce, or iceberg)
  • 2-3 tomato slices
  • Â¼ red onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise or mustard
  • 1 tablespoon butter, softened
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: pickles, bacon, avocado, or sprouts

Instructions

  1. Prepare bread: Toast bread slices if desired. Let them cool slightly.
  2. Spread base: On two bread slices, spread mayonnaise or mustard evenly.
  3. Layer ingredients: Add lettuce leaves, then tomato slices, and onion rings.
  4. Add meat and cheese: Layer deli meat and cheese slices on top of vegetables.
  5. Season: Add salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Complete sandwich: Top with remaining bread slices.
  7. Optional grilling: For a grilled sandwich, butter outside of bread and grill in pan until golden and cheese melts.
  8. Serve: Cut diagonally and serve immediately with pickles or chips.
