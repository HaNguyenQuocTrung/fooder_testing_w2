Instructions

  1. Prepare vegetables: If using vegetables, dice them small and sautÃ© in a little butter until tender. Set aside.
  2. Beat eggs: In a bowl, crack eggs and add milk if using. Beat with a fork until yolks and whites are combined but not frothy.
  3. Season eggs: Add salt and pepper to beaten eggs. Don't over-season as cheese will add saltiness.
  4. Heat pan: Heat an 8-inch non-stick skillet over medium heat. Add butter and swirl to coat.
  5. Cook eggs: Pour in eggs and let them set for about 30 seconds without stirring.
  6. Push edges: Using a spatula, gently push cooked edges toward center, tilting pan so uncooked egg runs underneath.
  7. Add fillings: When eggs are mostly set but still slightly wet on top, sprinkle cheese and vegetables over one half.
  8. Fold omelette: Carefully fold the empty half over the filled half using your spatula.
  9. Finish cooking: Cook for another 30 seconds to melt cheese completely.
  10. Serve immediately: Slide omelette onto plate and garnish with fresh herbs.
